Puttu & kadala

A spicy gravy based curry recipe made with black chickpeas and coconut. it is a purpose based curry and is served specifically with kerala puttu and hence it is also known as puttu kadala curry. it is very simple and easy to make and more importantly can also be served as a side dish to chapati and dal rice combination.
